    Eternally Stylish Dresses

    Whether you are 13 or 30, one thing all women can agree on is that dresses are an essential part of our wardrobe. Dresses have a unique appeal that can rarely be recreated by any other outfit. From vintage flower dresses to modern bodycon dresses, the styles have varied over the years.

    The history of dresses dates back to centuries ago when they were designed with puffy sleeves with crinolines under the skirt. Now you can find a variety of dresses from pretty prom dresses to trendy club dresses. Myntra has choices galore when it comes to dress for girls and women. Explore the expansive range of women and girls dresses on Myntra from premium brands.

    Types of dresses

    There is a dress for every occasion and season. Listed below are some of the popular types of dresses categorised based on different occasions:

  • Casual dresses- Want to feel chic and pretty without putting in too much effort? Then swap out your jeans and t-shirt for a casual dress. Dresses in cotton are perfect for everyday wear. For casual wear, choose from t-shirt style, shirt style, fit and flare, and blouson dresses.
  • Work dresses- More and more women are opting to wear dresses to work nowadays. Well-structured formal dresses make you look sharp and classy. A-line, peplum, and sheath dresses make for the perfect work-wear outfit. For winters, opt for sweater dresses with a pair of solid stockings or tights.
  • Party dresses- Nothing looks as glamorous as a shimmery bodycon dress for a night on the town with your friends. Party dresses come in a wide range of styles to suit different occasions. For formal parties and events, opt for cocktail dresses in full length made of satin, velvet, organza, or soft silk. Embellished maxi or Anarkali dresses are perfect for ethnic parties.

    • Tips on Styling Dresses

    Picking the right dress and knowing how to style it for different occasions will change your fashion game. Listed below are some tips and tricks on styling dresses:

  • For summer, opt for light cotton dresses with floral and fun prints. Off-shoulder, halter-neck, and strappy dresses in pastel colours make for the perfect summer dresses. Opt for a strapless bra when wearing off-shoulder and halter-neck dresses.
  • Style your shirt dress with a wide belt at the waist for a chic look.
  • When it comes to wedding dresses, opt for fabrics like organza, satin, crepe, taffeta, chiffon, and charmeuse. You can opt for a designer dress or take your pick from one of the premium brands.
  • Red dresses are perfect for date nights. Opt for a boatneck chiffon red dress and nude pumps for a perfect date night look.
  • For a form-fitting look, opt for bodycon dresses. Shapewear can help you achieve a smooth silhouette with these dresses.
  • Choose dresses that flatter your body type. If you are on the slimmer side, opt for wrap dresses to give the illusion of curves. If you are on the healthier side, opt for plus size dresses with a square or round neckline.

    Are dresses more comfortable during pregnancy?

    Maternity wear has become chic in recent years, and dresses are a welcome addition to the maternity wear range. These dresses are designed, keeping the expecting mother’s needs in mind and are very comfortable. Also, the designs are trendy, which makes them an excellent choice for different occasions

    Can Formal dresses be worn for an office presentation/Interview?

    Formal dresses are styled for corporate wear and are very much in vogue. They display an understated elegance and are perfect for any formal event such as an interview, meeting with clients or an official presentation.

    What type of dresses can be worn for receptions?

    Myntra has a curated range of dresses for the party as well as wedding receptions. The focus is to create the perfect balance between modern and traditional. Maxi dress, A-line, empire and kurti dress with embellishments, ethnic motifs or embroidery are popular choices in the reception wear range.

    How can we style dresses?

    Dress in itself makes a strong style statement which can be enhanced by pairing it with accessories, some amount of mix n match works really well.
  • A shirt dress can be styled with a broad belt at the waist.
  • A sleeveless plain maxi dress can be styled with an embellished jacket and some chunky jewellery.
  • A solid formal dress, peep toes and a delicate necklace create the perfect corporate look.
  • A denim dress with sneakers is ideal for a casual outing.
  • A scarf wrapped around a short printed dress with sandals is chic and comfortable attire.
